Teach Yourself How to Make a Tasty Chana Masala

Description

Chana masala is a tasty meal that combines tomatoes and chickpeas. The spices make your house smell wonderful on a cold winter day, though the flavors are rich enough to eat any time of the year.

Ingredients

  • onions
  • tomatoes
  • chickpeas
  • garam maasala
  • cinamon

Instructions

  1. Soak and boil the chickpeas.
  2. Heat 2 tablespoons of oil in a pan. Add 2 cloves and on tiny cinamon stick. Add one spoon of ginger garlic paste.
  3. Add half a onion and fry until translucent.
  4. Add 2 chopped tomatoes. Cover the pan and cook until the tomatoes are mushy.
  5. Transfer to a blender and blend until a smooth paste.
  6. Heat some oil or butter in a pan then transfer the sauce into the pan. Add one pinch turmeric powder, tablespoon chili powder, half tablespoon garam masala and one tablespoon chana masala. Salt as needed and cover and cook on medium heat for 5 minutes.
  7. Add the boile chickpeas and cook for a few more minutes.
  8. Serve with rice.
