Pretty Tasty Okra Stir Fry

Description

Smoky okra cooked with onions and spices. Can be tuned to be extra spicey. Careful when adding the chili powder that you don't fummigate yourself.

Ingredients

  • onions
  • okra
  • turmeric
  • chili powder
  • garam masala
  • cumin seeds

Instructions

  1. Heat 2-3 tablespoons of oil in a pan.
  2. Once the oil is hot add in 2 pinches of cumin seeds.
  3. Add chopped okra and mix well.
  4. Once most of the stickiness is gone add a small cup of finely chopped onions and fry until translucent.
  5. Add a pinch of turmeric powder, one pinch of garam masala, salt as needed, and chili powder. Mix it well with onions, otherwise it will burn.
  6. Add another 2 tablespoons of oil and finish by frying for another few minutes.
  7. Serve with rice. Takes well with lentil curry.
